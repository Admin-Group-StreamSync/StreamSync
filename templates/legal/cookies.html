Cookies Policy

1. What are cookies and why are they used?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on the user's device when they visit this platform. They are used to ensure the correct functioning of the service, improve the user experience and analyze the use of the platform.

2. Types of used cookies

The following types of cookies may be used on this platform:

  • Session cookies: They are temporary and are automatically deleted when the user closes the browser. They are used to keep the session active during browsing.
  • Persistent cookies: They remain for a certain period of time and allow the user's preferences or settings to be remembered on future visits.

3. Managing and disabling cookies

The user can configure their browser to accept, reject or delete cookies at any time. Disabling cookies may affect the correct functioning of some functionalities of the platform.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

4. Relationship with the Privacy Policy

For more information on the processing of personal data, please consult the Privacy Policy.
